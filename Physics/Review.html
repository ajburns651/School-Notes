Equations

  • Torque

    \(\tau=Fr\sin(\theta)\)

    \(I=kmR^2\)

  • Connecting

    \(x=R\theta\)
    \(v=R\omega\)
    \(a=R\alpha\)

  • General

    \(\theta = \frac12 \alpha t^2\)
    \(\omega_f^2=\omega^2 + 2\alpha \theta\)
    \(\alpha = \omega / t\)
    \(\omega = \theta /t\)
